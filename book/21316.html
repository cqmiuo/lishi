It is designed to impart to the reader the mathematical techniques used in modelling signals and systems, encompassing standard mathematical tools as well as newer techniques such as wavelets and neural networks. C++ and Java implementations furnish these de*ions. The book offers an excellent balance of theory and application, beginning with a complete framework of discrete-time signal processing.
