1．The Name of Mummy

A little girl can’t find her way home. When she is crying, a policeman ask her, “who is with you?” The girl answer, “ Mummy.” Then the policeman ask, “what is her name?” The girl answer, “well, she has several names. I call her Mummy, Dad calls her Honey. Granny calls her Jenny.”
