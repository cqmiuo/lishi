内容简介：

　　A brand new 'Smart Guide' from Insight, featuring an A-Z

format that allows you to find your way around the book with ease

and plan your trip according to your very own personal taste.

Themed headings cover over 400 things to see and do from

architecture, history, cafes and hotels, to nightlife, restaurants,

bars, shopping and much more, allowing you to set your own

priorities for a truly tailor-made and individual travel

experience. The front-cover flap highlights all the top attractions

and pinpoints exactly where to find these within the guide, for

quick and easy reference at a glance. Area overviews conveniently

highlight the very best each district has to offer, from the old

Medina to the 'Ville Nouvelle' of Gueliz, through the Mouassine

Quarter and the Menara. The guide also contains a transport map and

10-page street atlas with full index and detailed mapping,

cross-referenced to the main text for easy navigation. The

back-cover flap provides on-the-spot practical information, such as

important telephone numbers and websites, useful phrases and a

climate chart, not to mention also making a handy bookmark. Smart

Guides - the smart way to navigate.
