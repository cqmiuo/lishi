内容简介：

This is primarily a textbook for graduate and upper-level

undergraduate students of law. However, practising lawyers and

policy-makers who are looking for an introduction to WTO law will

also find it invaluable. The book covers both the institutional and

substantive law of the WTO. While the treatment of the law is often

quite detailed, the main aim of this textbook is to make clear the

basic principles and underlying logic of WTO law and the world

trading system. Each section contains questions and assignments, to

allow students to assess their understanding and develop useful

practical skills. At the end of each chapter there is a helpful

summary, as well as an exercise on specific, true-to-life

international trade problems.
