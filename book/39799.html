书籍介绍

A rebranded edition of this acclaimed dictionary -- the ideal choice for beginners of any age. This first edition of the market-leading Collins Easy Learning Italian Dictionary provides beginners of Italian with the easiest way to learn the language. Its features include full coverage of core vocabulary in both Italian and English， thousands of examples to show how to use the translations and all essential set expressions. The layout is clear and exceptionally user-friendly， using colour for the words you need to look up， and a new line for each translation. The dictionary also offers additional help in the form of a full guide to using the dictionary， useful grammar pages， a section on expressing yourself in Italian， and activities and games for developing dictionary skills. It also lists all the most common， and most dangerous "false friends" to help prevent very common mistakes beginners make when speaking Italian. Easy to read， easy to use and easy to understand， the new edition of the Easy Learning Italian Dictionary is the ideal choice for beginners of any age.
