Chinese interior design is a kaleidoscope of competing influences: scholarly gardens and opium dens, imperial palaces alongside concrete-and-steel high rises and rural simplicity contrasting with urban chaos. Photographed in locations as diverse as Shanghai, New York, Hong Kong and London, China Style (Periplus Editions) reveals how Chinese tradition is being reinterpreted to produce fresh and dynamic design ideas.
