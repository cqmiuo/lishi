Betty Barnden is a freelance needlecraft designer who studied furniture design at the Royal College of Art. Her embroidery, knitwear and crochet designs have appeared in many magazines over the years including Woman, Woman's Own, Woman's Weekly, Prima, Classic Stitches, Beautiful Stitches, Popular Crafts, Sewing World and Inspirations. She has designed embroidery projects and kits for Coats Craft UK and The Craft Collection. Her knitting and crochet patterns have been published by yarn companies such as Sirdar, Patons, Wendy Wools and Twilleys of Stamford, and she has contributed designs to books such as Wild Knitting, The Knit Kit and The Sweater Book. --This text refers to an out of print or unavailable edition of this title.
